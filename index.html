Risk Analysis

Addressed Before You Ask

Low league participation

Start with 1 league (padel, 32 players). Grow from demand. 8 courts = capacity to scale 4× with zero infrastructure. Conservative model assumes only 60% of capacity.

Championship sponsorship shortfall

Run lean Year 1 (AED 500K budget). Build the track record. Sponsors invest in Year 2+ based on proven content and attendance. Conservative model excludes title sponsor in Year 1.

Longevity clinic licensing

Start with non-medical services (IV therapy, cryo, testing) while DHA licensing processes. These are the highest-margin offerings. Full medical launches Year 2.

Nightlife noise management

Professional acoustic engineering built into the AED 605K sound budget. Funktion-One at 150 capacity = world-class sound without venue-level volume. Acoustic isolation in the build spec.

Economic downturn

Premium proved resilient in 2020-2022. League + health data = sticky retention. 12+ revenue streams reduce single-source dependency. Freehold asset holds value regardless.

Revenue projections too aggressive

Even the conservative scenario (60%) adds AED 4.2M. The full projection adds AED 7M. Every scenario is profitable because the scope is focused and setup costs are under AED 800K.
